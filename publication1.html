An illustration of a two-action block stacking task and possible success/failure outcomes.
(a) Initial task state: three blocks are laid out on the table.
(b) Intermediate task state: two blocks form a partially-built stable tower.
(c) Task success: all blocks are placed into a stable tower.
(d) Task failure: improper block placement causes the tower to topple.
